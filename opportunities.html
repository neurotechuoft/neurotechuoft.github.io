Executive Positions

Vice President—Operations

Keep the club afloat! Ensure that the club is able to drive undergrad neurotech innovation.

  • Ensure NeurotechUofT remains recognized as a ULife, UTSU, and EngSoc club
  • Advise members on financial position of the group
  • Prepare an annual budget for the group, and sign off with the President on budgets for specific initiatives
  • Make and maintain a contact list of all registered members
  • Book all rooms for meetings and events
  • Ensure accountability from club executives using CCR
  • Enter CCR roles in the CCR database and be the primary liaison between members and CCR validator

Hours per week: 6—8 hours
